__General__
**poll** "[question]" [option_1] "[option 2]" ... [option_N] - *Creates a 1-minute live poll*
**timedPoll** [duration] "[question]" [option_1] ... [option_N] - *Creates a live poll with a custom timer specified in __*minutes*__

__Quote__
**addQuote** [text] - *Adds a string of text as a quote*
**quoteThat** - *Adds the last message in the current channel as a quote*
**quoteID** [message_ID] - *Adds a quote from a message ID*
**deleteQuote** [number] - *Deletes a quote*
**randomQoute** - *Shows a random quote in this server*
**quotes** - *Shows all quotes in this server*
**by** [user] - *Shows quotes by a specific user in this server*

__Bot__
**ping** - *Shows bot latency in milliseconds*
**play** [game] - *Changes the game status of the bot*
**status** [status] - *Changes the status of the bot: `online`, `idle`, `dnd`*

*Note: case insensitive*
